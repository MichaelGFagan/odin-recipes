Chicken Nuggets

Description

Frozen chicken nuggets because I don't want to come up with more original content.

Ingredients

  • A bag of frozen chicken nuggets
  • Some oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Place a strip of aluminum foil on a baking sheet. Lightly coat foil with oil.
  3. Place desired amount of nuggets on pan.
  4. Place in oven for 12 minutes. Flip each nugget. Return nuggets to oven.
  5. Remove nuggets from oven after 10 minutes. Serve.
